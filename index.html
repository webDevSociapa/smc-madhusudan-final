Why Choose Us

Farm-to-Freezer Freshness

We harvest at peak ripeness, flash-freeze within minutes using IQF technology, and guarantee nutrient retention and taste superior to conventional freezing methods.

Uncompromising
Safety

Rigorous HACCP and ISO 22000 protocols, continuous monitoring, and third-party audits ensure every batch which meets to the highest global food-safety standards.

Sustainable
Practices

We invest in effluent treatment, solar power integration, and water recycling to minimize our environmental footprint and support long-term agricultural health.

Our vertically integrated campus features six IQF freezing lines with 12 MT/hour capacity each, a 20,000 MT cold-storage facility, a 300 KLD effluent treatment plant, and in-house R&D lab for continuous product innovation and quality assurance.
