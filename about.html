About SMC Group

SMC Agri Limited is part of the larger SMC Group, a multifaceted conglomerate with interests spanning dairy, agriculture, and food processing.

Over three decades, SMC Group has built an integrated ecosystem—from farm inputs to value-added products—leveraging cutting-edge technology and community-driven initiatives that empower farmers and ensure consistent quality.
